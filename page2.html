About


Click here to find out a bit of our history and tradition


This pizzeria originated in Petrópolis, Brazil and came to be known as one of the finest restaurants in town. The first restaurant proprietor is believed to have been an aristocrat, a known cheese vendor, who started his business in in 1978.

What started as a small business is nowadays one of the biggest chains in the country with its 17 branches spreaded in national territory. In "Pizza Loca" we are proud of our homemade doughes and sauces that remains kept as a family secret recipe over all these years.



The italian family that founded the first restaurant was composed of four members: two daughters, the father and the mamma. The mamma was a talented cook, she would prepare the pizzas herself, while the father as a cheese vendor would provide the best quality cheese of the whole area. The daughters would help the family business by announcing it to the outlanders that would arrive in the city.
