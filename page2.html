About us




Click here to find out a bit about our history and tradition


Our pizzeria originated in Petrópolis, Brazil and came to be known as one of the finest restaurants in town. The first restaurant proprietor is believed to have been an aristocrat, a known cheese vendor, who started his business in 1957.

What started as a small business is now one of the biggest chains in the country with its 17 branches spreaded in national territory. In Mamma Mia we are proud of our homemade dough and sauces that are a family secret recipe that has been passed over all these years.





The family that founded the first restaurant was composed of four members: two twin daughters, the father and the mamma. The mamma was a talented cook, she would prepare the pizzas herself, while the father as a cheese vendor would provide the best quality cheese of the whole area. The daughters would help the family business grow by announcing it to the travellers that would arrive in the outskirts.
